DATOM: Leverage data to create business value

Origin

This method is developed by Clement Levallois, Associate Professor at emlyon business school and Guillaume Lecuyer, Product Marketing Director at Visiativ Software.

A French version of the method is available -> here <- .

DATOM is a canvas-based method guiding managers to design business projects where data plays a key role in generating value for the end user. DATOM also ensures the project is in line with the strategic objectives of the organization.

DATOM is for you to use without restriction in modeling your own or other people's businesses. If you wish to use the DATOM in original or adapted form to sell it as a tool, you must contact the copyright holders, Clement Levallois and Guillaume Lecuyer, for permission.
