DATOM: Leverage data to create business value

Origin

This method is developed by Clement Levallois, Associate Professor at emlyon business school and Guilaume Lecuyer, Product Marketing Director at Visiativ Software. A French version of the method is available soon.

DATOM is a canvas-based method guiding managers to design business projects where data plays a key role in generating value for the end user. DATOM also ensures the project is in line with the strategic objectives of the organization.
